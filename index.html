Why S2R Works
Six operating principles
Principle 1
Logic before process
Most strategy fails from errors of logic — not errors of data. Thinking quality comes first.
Principle 2
Strategy is iterative
Not an annual event. A living feedback loop: sense → interpret → decide → act → observe.
Principle 3
All four Ws matter
WHAT, WHY, HOW and WHEN must all be explicit. Any gap creates structural incompleteness.
Principle 4
Value must be complete
Creating value is not enough. It must be delivered and the system that delivers it must be sustained.
Principle 5
Context is non-negotiable
Every strategy is a position inside a specific environment. Generic strategy is no strategy.
Principle 6
Grounding makes it durable
Culture, ESRG and environmental fit are not soft additions — they are the conditions of long-term strategy.
